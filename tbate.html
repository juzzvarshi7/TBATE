Story Summary

The Beginning After The End follows the life of the late King Grey after his untimely and mysterious death. Reborn as Arthur Leywin, he seeks to correct his past mistakes in the vibrant new continent of Dicathen, a world of magic and fantastical creatures. Equipped with the knowledge of a powerful king in his mid-thirties, Arthur navigates his new life as the magic-wielding child of two retired adventurers and gains purpose through each of his new experiences—something he lacked in his previous life. When a kind dragon sacrifices her life to protect him, Arthur resolves to live a sincere, kind, and courageous life with those he loves. With the help of a lost elf princess and the Elven Kingdom of Elenoir, Arthur begins his long journey to find his true place in the world.

As the years pass, Arthur becomes more and more comfortable in this world, positioning himself as a young, but respected figure. However, deja-vu strikes as a war brews between Dicathen and the Vritra, a clan of banished deities now ruling over a faraway continent. Arthur must rise as a leader, despite his fear of becoming the war-hardened monster he once was in his past life. As the war rages on, Arthur discovers that he was not reborn to this world by chance… nor was he the only one.

Main Characters

Arthur Leywin

Arthur Leywin is the reincarnated King Grey. Equipped with the knowledge of a powerful king in his mid-thirties, Arthur navigates his new life as the magic-wielding child of two retired adventurers. He seeks to correct his past mistakes and live a sincere, kind, and courageous life. Arthur becomes a young but respected figure as he positions himself in the world of Dicathen. Despite his fear of becoming the war-hardened monster he once was, Arthur rises as a leader as a war brews between Dicathen and the Vritra.

Sylvie

Sylvie is the daughter of Sylvia Indrath and Agrona Vritra, as well as the granddaughter of Kezess Indrath and Myre. She is the bond of Arthur Leywin, who was entrusted to him by her mother Sylvia. After she completes her training with Kezess Indrath, she participates in the war with Arthur until the war is lost. Later, she uses a powerful Aether Art to save Arthur from dying, losing her physical body in the process. Arthur works to revive her by filling the egg with Aether.

After Arthur succeeds in reviving her with the help of the pantheon Asura Aldir and the phoenix Asura Mordain, it is revealed that she and Arthur were always bound by fate. During Arthur's reincarnation, she caught his soul, preventing it from falling in Agrona's dominion and making him a Leywin, giving him a second chance in his new life in Dicathen.
